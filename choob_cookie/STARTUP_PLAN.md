# Choob Cookie — Small‑Scale Launch Ideas

## Philosophy
Keep it personal, preserve the love behind the recipe, and grow organically within the neighborhood and community.

---

## 1. Start Small & Local

### Direct‑to‑Neighbors
- **Pre‑order via WhatsApp / Text / Google Form** – Keep it simple. Neighbors can place orders for fresh batches.
- **Pickup or Local Delivery** – Offer pickup at yourmom’s house or drop‑off within a 2‑mile radius.

### Sample Boxes
- Give out free sample boxes (4‑6 cookies) to neighbors, include a small card with a QR code linking to the story.
- Ask for feedback – this helps perfect the product and builds word‑of‑mouth.

---

## 2. Partner with Community Spots

- **Local coffee shops / tea houses** – Offer cookies as a complimentary side or sell on consignment.
- **Community centers / libraries** – Set up a small “cookie table” during events.
- **School & church bake sales** – Perfect for small‑scale exposure.

---

## 3. Simple Packaging

- Use eco‑friendly packaging: small recyclable bags or kraft boxes.
- Include a **handwritten note** from yourmom thanking them for trying the cookies.
- Add a **QR code** linking to the story (see QR_STORY.md).

---

## 4. Micro‑Brand Social Presence

- **Instagram / TikTok** – Share the legacy, behind‑the‑scenes clips of baking, and stories from family members.
- Post a short video of yourmom rolling the dough, the aroma, the finished cookies.
- Use a simple hashtag like #ChoobCookieLove or #GrandmasTeaCookie.

---

## 5. Keep It Artisanal

- **Batch‑by‑Batch**: Only bake a set number of cookies per week (e.g., 50–100) to maintain quality and avoid overwhelm.
- **Limited‑Time Offers**: Release “special edition” flavors once a month to create excitement.
- **subscription Box**: Offer a monthly box for repeat customers – keeps the connection alive.

---

## 6. Preserve the Legacy

- **Storytelling First**: Always lead with grandma’s story – the QR code does this.
- **Secret Recipe Protection**: Keep the exact recipe in the family; only the family knows the exact measurements. Share only the process, not the exact ratios.
- **Family Involvement**: Have family members help with packaging, delivery, and community outreach – it keeps the love tangible.

---

## Quick Start Checklist

- [ ] Set up a simple Google Form for pre‑orders (name, qty, pickup/delivery).
- [ ] Design a basic label with QR code and yourmom’s thank‑you note.
- [ ] Bake an initial batch of 30–50 cookies for neighbors.
- [ ] Share on personal Instagram/TikTok – add the story link in bio.
- [ ] Ask for feedback and adjust recipe if needed.

---

*Let's keep the love in every bite! 🍪*
