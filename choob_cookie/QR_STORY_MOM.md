# The Choob Cookie Story

My grandma was a baker, event planner, and homemaker, but her greatest magic was a secret tea cookie she'd roll into rounds, sprinkle with sesame seeds, and share with everyone who walked through her door as her way of saying "I love you, take a piece of my heart with you." When we moved to America, she kept baking and I kept watching, learning every movement. After she passed, I found myself in the same kitchen doing the same thing, realizing the recipe was hers but the love in it had become mine. Now everyone who tries them says I should sell them — maybe someday I will, but for now I just want to share a piece of our story with you because these cookies are more than a snack, they're a hug from my kitchen to yours.
