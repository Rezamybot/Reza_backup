# The Choob Cookie Story

My grandma could do it all — baker, event planner, homemaker — but her greatest magic was a secret tea cookie she'd roll into perfect rounds, sprinkle with sesame seeds, and share with everyone who walked through her door. She called it her way of saying "I love you, take a piece of my heart with you." When we moved to America, she kept baking and I kept watching, learning every movement by heart. After she passed, I found myself in the same kitchen, doing the exact same thing, and I realized the recipe was hers but the love in it had become mine. Now I make these cookies for my own family, and everyone who tries them tells me I should sell them — maybe someday I will, but for now I just want to share a piece of our story with you. These cookies are more than a snack, they're a hug from my kitchen to yours. This is a tradition I want to keep spreading, one cookie at a time.
