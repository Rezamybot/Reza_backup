# Choob Cookie — Project Background

## The Legacy
This project is dedicated to Mana's grandma, who passed away 2 years ago. She was an active woman in Tehran, Iran during the 60s and 70s, working as a baker, event planner, homemaker, and bride/bridal party decorator. She was always learning, doing, empowering, and passing on her knowledge to younger girls growing up.

## The Secret Recipe
- **Tea Cookie**: A special recipe that grandma kept secret
- She would make it for family members, relatives, and visitors as parting gifts
- Later brought the recipe to the US and continued making it for family and close neighbors

## The Next Generation
- The recipe has been passed down to her daughter (Mana's mom)
- Mom has perfected the recipe
- She continues to share the love by making the tea biscuit for her kids and families

## The Opportunity
Everyone who tries it always tells her she should start selling it and manufacturing it.
