# DaySprout - Project Summary

## What Is DaySprout?
AI-powered personalized video messages delivered daily (or on-demand) to loved ones. Think of it as an AI avatar that delivers a custom "good morning" or "thinking of you" video — personalized with the recipient's name and your message.

## Why This Business?
- **Unique** — No one does daily AI video wishes at scale
- **Viral-worthy** — People share these, generating free marketing
- **Passive income** — Make once, sell forever
- **Scalable** — One video template, infinite personalization
- **We have HeyGen** — Already connected and ready

## Budget
- $100/month for 3 months = $300 total
- Most costs near $0 (HeyGen API, Gumroad/Etsy free to list)
- Possible minor ads: ~$50-75/month if needed

## Target Audience
- People who want to surprise friends/family with thoughtful messages
- Long-distance couples, separated families, military families
- Niche: birthday mornings, "just because" days, encouragement

## Revenue Model
- Per-video pricing: $5-15 per personalized video
- Bundle packages: 5 videos for $40, 10 for $70
- Subscription model later: $15/month for daily videos

4. Create landing page
