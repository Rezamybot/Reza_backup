# DOTKIT — Period Kit for Schools

**By Kayla, 6th Grade**

---

## What is DOTKIT?

DOTKIT is a free period kit designed for young girls who need emergency supplies at school. Every kit includes:

- ✅ Disposable underwear
- ✅ Pads of different sizes
- ✅ Panty liner
- ✅ Disposal bag (for soiled underwear)
- ✅ Information card (how to use the products)

---

## Who is it for?

Girls in 5th grade and up who are becoming of age and getting their period. Many girls miss school because they don't have period products handy — DOTKIT changes that.

---

## How it works?

1. **Stocked**: Kits are stored at the school nurse's office
2. **Distributed**: When a girl needs supplies, she can grab a kit — no questions asked, no embarrassment
3. **Restocked**: I will check in with the school nurse **once a month** to see if supplies need to be refilled

---

## Why this matters to me?

When I got my period for the first time, it caught me off guard. I didn't have supplies, and I was too embarrassed to ask for help. But I decided then — I didn't want any other girl to go through that alone.

DOTKIT is for every girl who deserves to feel prepared, not embarrassed.

## Why this matters?

- No girl should miss school because of her period
- Kits are discreet, easy to use, and come with simple instructions
- I'm a kid just like them — this is led by a teen, for teens
- I decided to do something about it — and so can every girl who needs help
